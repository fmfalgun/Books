## PREPARATION TIPS FOR GENERAL AWARENESS:

1. **Daily News Reading:** Spend 30 minutes daily on The Hindu, Indian Express, or BBC News
2. **Defence Focus:** Read about Indian military, strategic developments, global conflicts
3. **Monthly Revision:** Create revision notes on current affairs every month
4. **Static Knowledge:** Build strong foundation in history, geography, Indian polity
5. **Science & Technology:** Follow ISRO, space missions, latest tech developments
6. **Sports Tracking:** Keep updated on major sporting events and Indian athletes
7. **Current Cutoff:** Focus on developments from past 12-18 months
8. **Source Management:** Use 2-3 reliable news sources consistently

**Critical Areas for AFCAT:**
- Indian Air Force developments and defence budget
- Global military conflicts and geopolitics
- Recent space missions and scientific breakthroughs
- Government schemes and policy changes
- Constitutional matters and governance

**Total Practice Time:** 55-60 minutes for full coverage
