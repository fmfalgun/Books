**Days 11-15: General Awareness**
- Read daily news (The Hindu, BBC)
- Solve 20 GA questions with focus
- Revise history, geography, polity

**Days 16-20: Reasoning Section**
- Practice verbal reasoning (analogies, coding)
- Practice non-verbal reasoning (patterns, cubes)
- Solve 15-20 questions daily

### Phase 3: Integration & Speed Building (Week 5-6)
**Days 21-25: Mixed Practice**
- Attempt 2-3 questions from each section daily
- Build speed while maintaining accuracy
- Practice under timed conditions (2 minutes per question average)

**Days 26-30: Full-Length Mock Tests**
- Take complete 100-question test in 120 minutes
- Analyze weak areas
- Targeted revision based on mistakes
